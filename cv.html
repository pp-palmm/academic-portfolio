Cover Letter

Introduction

My name is Phuriphan Prathipasen. I hold a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence from Sirindhorn International Institute of Technology (SIIT), Thammasat University, Thailand, and I currently work as a Data and AI Transformation Consultant, serving as the lead of the data and AI strategy team. During my undergraduate studies, I had the opportunity to participate in the Young Science and Engineering Researchers Program (YSEP) at Tokyo Institute of Technology, where I conducted NLP research under Tokunaga Laboratory, supervised by Professor Tokunaga Takenobu. My research there focused on the semantics alignment of idea units using large language models, investigating how effectively LLMs can align idea units between source texts and student summaries as part of a summarization task. I have also contributed to 2 journal articles and 3 international conference publications in natural language processing, machine learning, and deep learning, both as a research assistant at my university and as an intern at Thailand's National Electronics and Computer Technology Center (NECTEC). One of my NLP journal publications focused on music processing for composer classification, where we represented musical pieces using SentencePiece and Word2vec to classify composers, achieving a high F1-score of 1.00 on classical music data, under the supervision of Professor Virach Sornlertlamvanich and Dr. Somrudee Deepaisarn.

Alongside my academic work, I co-founded a music streaming startup during university that helped users relax and stay productive through a customizable listening environment. The platform grew to over 5,000 users and won awards and top rankings across more than 11 startup competitions, such as AWS Entrepreneurship Program, Startup Thailand League 2021 and 2022, and TED Fellow Youth Startup Fund 2021, where we placed as winners or top-ranked finalists in each.

Since graduating, I have accumulated over 2.5 years of professional experience across two companies as a Data and AI Transformation Consultant, working at the intersection of deep technical implementation and strategic advisory. In this role, I have led data and AI strategy engagements by developing and implementing data and AI strategy frameworks, generating actionable use cases, and building data and AI roadmaps that guide organisations through their transformation journey. I have also led client meetings to assess business challenges and opportunities, facilitated ideation workshops, designed solution architectures, crafted technical proposals, and managed cross-functional teams across data science, data engineering, and product development. On the technical side, I have written data pipelines, implemented data visualizations, and built LLM applications, giving me firsthand experience of the exact systems my research aims to improve. Working directly with C-level executives and seeing products through from concept to delivery has given me a clear and grounded view of where AI systems succeed and where they break down in practice, which is what ultimately drives my research motivation.

Having built my foundation across both academic research and industry practice, I have come to deeply understand the gap between what AI systems can do in theory and what they reliably deliver in practice. I have seen firsthand how LLM-based systems that work well in controlled settings often break down in real production environments. This observation is what drives my motivation to pursue research that is not just academically meaningful but also directly applicable to the real-world challenges that businesses and users face today.
